### 1.5 ArrayedCollection {#arrayedcollection .section}

[]{#index-ArrayedCollection}

**Defined in namespace Smalltalk**\
**Superclass: SequenceableCollection**\
**Category: Collections-Sequenceable**

:   My instances are objects that are generally fixed size, and are
    accessed by an integer index. The ordering of my instance's elements
    is determined externally; I will not rearrange the order of the
    elements.
