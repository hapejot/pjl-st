#### 1.9.18 Behavior: printing hierarchy {#behavior-printing-hierarchy .subsection}

[]{#index-hierarchyIndent} []{#index-printHierarchy-1}

**hierarchyIndent**

Answer the indent to be used by #printHierarchy - 4 by default

[]{#index-printFullHierarchy}

**printFullHierarchy**

Print my full hierarchy (i.e. all my superclasses and subclasses) on the
terminal.

[]{#index-printHierarchy}

**printHierarchy**

Print my entire subclass hierarchy on the terminal.
