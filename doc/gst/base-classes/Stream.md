### 1.157 Stream {#stream .section}

[]{#index-Stream}

**Defined in namespace Smalltalk**\
**Superclass: Iterable**\
**Category: Streams**

:   I am an abstract class that provides interruptable sequential access
    to objects. I can return successive objects from a source, or accept
    successive objects and store them sequentially on a sink. I provide
    some simple iteration over the contents of one of my instances, and
    provide for writing collections sequentially.
