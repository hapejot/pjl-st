#### 1.46.1 CScalarCType: accessing {#cscalarctype-accessing .subsection}

[]{#index-valueType}

**valueType**

valueType is used as a means to communicate to the interpreter the
underlying type of the data. For scalars, it is supplied by the CObject
subclass.
