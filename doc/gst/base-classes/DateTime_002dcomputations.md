[]{#DateTime_003a-computations}

#### 1.60.5 DateTime: computations {#datetime-computations .subsection}

[]{#index-asSeconds-1}

**asSeconds**

Answer the date as the number of seconds from 1/1/1901.

[]{#index-dayOfWeek-1}

**dayOfWeek**

Answer the day of week of the receiver. Unlike Dates, DateAndTimes have
1 = Sunday, 7 = Saturday

[]{#index-hour}

**hour**

Answer the hour in a 24-hour clock

[]{#index-hour12}

**hour12**

Answer the hour in a 12-hour clock

[]{#index-hour24}

**hour24**

Answer the hour in a 24-hour clock

[]{#index-meridianAbbreviation} []{#index-AM} []{#index-PM}

**meridianAbbreviation**

Answer either #AM (for anti-meridian) or #PM (for post-meridian)

[]{#index-minute}

**minute**

Answer the minute

[]{#index-second}

**second**

Answer the month represented by the receiver
