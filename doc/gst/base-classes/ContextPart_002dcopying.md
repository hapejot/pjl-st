#### 1.41.5 ContextPart: copying {#contextpart-copying .subsection}

[]{#index-copyStack}

**copyStack**

Answer a copy of the entire stack.

[]{#index-deepCopy-4}

**deepCopy**

Answer a copy of the entire stack, but don't copy any of the other
instance variables of the context.
