#### 1.148.2 ScaledDecimal: arithmetic {#scaleddecimal-arithmetic .subsection}

[]{#index-_002a-10}

**\* aNumber**

Multiply two numbers and answer the result.

[]{#index-_002b-14}

**+ aNumber**

Sum two numbers and answer the result.

[]{#index-_002d-14}

**- aNumber**

Subtract aNumber from the receiver and answer the result.

[]{#index-_002f-10}

**/ aNumber**

Divide two numbers and answer the result.

[]{#index-_002f_002f-5}

**// aNumber**

Answer the integer quotient after dividing the receiver by aNumber with
truncation towards negative infinity.

[]{#index-_005c_005c-4}

**\\\\ aNumber**

Answer the remainder after integer division the receiver by aNumber with
truncation towards negative infinity.
