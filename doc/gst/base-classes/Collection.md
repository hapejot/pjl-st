### 1.37 Collection {#collection .section}

[]{#index-Collection}

**Defined in namespace Smalltalk**\
**Superclass: Iterable**\
**Category: Collections**

:   I am an abstract class. My instances are collections of objects. My
    subclasses may place some restrictions or add some definitions to
    how the objects are stored or organized; I say nothing about this. I
    merely provide some object creation and access routines for general
    collections of objects.
