### 1.150 Semaphore {#semaphore .section}

[]{#index-Semaphore}

**Defined in namespace Smalltalk**\
**Superclass: LinkedList**\
**Category: Language-Processes**

:   My instances represent counting semaphores. I provide methods for
    signalling the semaphore's availability, and methods for waiting for
    its availability. I also provide some methods for implementing
    critical sections.
