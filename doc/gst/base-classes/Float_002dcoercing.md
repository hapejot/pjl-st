#### 1.80.6 Float: coercing {#float-coercing .subsection}

[]{#index-asExactFraction}

**asExactFraction**

Convert the receiver into a fraction with optimal approximation, but
with usually huge terms.

[]{#index-asFraction}

**asFraction**

Convert the receiver into a fraction with a good (but undefined)
approximation

[]{#index-truncated}

**truncated**

Convert the receiver to an Integer. Only used for LargeIntegers, there
are primitives for the other cases.
