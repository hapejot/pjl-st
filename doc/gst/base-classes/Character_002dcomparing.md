#### 1.28.8 Character: comparing {#character-comparing .subsection}

[]{#index-_003c}

**\< aCharacter**

Compare the character's ASCII value. Answer whether the receiver's is
the least.

[]{#index-_003c_003d}

**\<= aCharacter**

Compare the character's ASCII value. Answer whether the receiver's is
the least or their equal.

[]{#index-_003e}

**\> aCharacter**

Compare the character's ASCII value. Answer whether the receiver's is
the greatest.

[]{#index-_003e_003d}

**\>= aCharacter**

Compare the character's ASCII value. Answer whether the receiver's is
the greatest or their equal.
