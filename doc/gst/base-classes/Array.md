### 1.4 Array {#array .section}

[]{#index-Array}

**Defined in namespace Smalltalk**\
**Superclass: ArrayedCollection**\
**Category: Collections-Sequenceable**

:   My instances are objects that have array-like properties: they are
    directly indexable by integers starting at 1, and they are fixed in
    size. I inherit object creation behavior messages such as #with:, as
    well as iteration and general access behavior from
    SequenceableCollection.
