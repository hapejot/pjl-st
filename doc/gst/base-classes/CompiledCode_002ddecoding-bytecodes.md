#### 1.39.8 CompiledCode: decoding bytecodes {#compiledcode-decoding-bytecodes .subsection}

[]{#index-dispatchTo_003awith_003a}

**dispatchTo: anObject with: param**

Disassemble the bytecodes and tell anObject about them in the form of
message sends. param is given as an argument to every message send.
