#### 1.132.4 Point: comparing {#point-comparing .subsection}

[]{#index-_003c-11}

**\< aPoint**

Answer whether the receiver is higher and to the left of aPoint

[]{#index-_003c_003d-8}

**\<= aPoint**

Answer whether aPoint is equal to the receiver, or the receiver is
higher and to the left of aPoint

[]{#index-_003d-34}

**= aPoint**

Answer whether the receiver is equal to aPoint

[]{#index-_003e-8}

**\> aPoint**

Answer whether the receiver is lower and to the right of aPoint

[]{#index-_003e_003d-8}

**\>= aPoint**

Answer whether aPoint is equal to the receiver, or the receiver is lower
and to the right of aPoint

[]{#index-max_003a-3}

**max: aPoint**

Answer self if it is lower and to the right of aPoint, aPoint otherwise

[]{#index-min_003a-3}

**min: aPoint**

Answer self if it is higher and to the left of aPoint, aPoint otherwise
