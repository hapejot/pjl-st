### 1.38 BLOX.BRadioGroup {#blox.bradiogroup .section}

[]{#index-BLOX_002eBRadioGroup}

**Defined in namespace BLOX**\
**Superclass: BLOX.BContainer**\
**Category: Graphics-Windows**

:   I am used to group many mutually-exclusive radio buttons together.
    In addition, just like every BContainer I can perform simple
    management by putting widgets next to each other, from left to right
    or (which is more useful in this particular case\...) from top to
    bottom.
