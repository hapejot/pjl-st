#### 5.18.2 I18N.LcNumeric: printing {#i18n.lcnumeric-printing .subsection}

[]{#index-basicPrint_003aon_003a}

**basicPrint: aNumber on: aStream**

Print aNumber on aStream according to the receiver's formatting
conventions, without currency signs or anything like that. This method
must not be overridden.

[]{#index-print_003aon_003a-3}

**print: aNumber on: aStream**

Print aNumber on aStream according to the receiver's formatting
conventions.
