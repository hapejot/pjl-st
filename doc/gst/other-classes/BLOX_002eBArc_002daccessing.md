[]{#BLOX_002eBArc_003a-accessing}

#### 1.1.1 BLOX.BArc: accessing {#blox.barc-accessing .subsection}

[]{#index-endAngle}

**endAngle**

Answer the ending of the angular range that is occupied by the arc,
expressed in degrees

[]{#index-endAngle_003a}

**endAngle: angle**

Set the ending of the angular range that is occupied by the arc,
expressed in degrees

[]{#index-fillChord}

**fillChord**

Specify that the arc will be filled by painting an area delimited by the
arc and the chord that joins the arc's endpoints.

[]{#index-fillSlice}

**fillSlice**

Specify that the arc will be filled by painting an area delimited by the
arc and the two radii joins the center of the arc with each of the
endpoints (that is, that a pie slice will be drawn).

[]{#index-from}

**from**

Answer the starting point of the arc in cartesian coordinates

[]{#index-from_003a}

**from: aPoint**

Set the starting point of the arc in cartesian coordinates

[]{#index-from_003ato_003a}

**from: start to: end**

Set the two starting points of the arc in cartesian coordinates

[]{#index-startAngle}

**startAngle**

Answer the beginning of the angular range that is occupied by the arc,
expressed in degrees

[]{#index-startAngle_003a}

**startAngle: angle**

Set the beginning of the angular range that is occupied by the arc,
expressed in degrees

[]{#index-sweepAngle}

**sweepAngle**

Answer the size of the angular range that is occupied by the arc,
expressed in degrees

[]{#index-sweepAngle_003a}

**sweepAngle: angle**

Set the size of the angular range that is occupied by the arc, expressed
in degrees

[]{#index-to}

**to**

Answer the ending point of the arc in cartesian coordinates

[]{#index-to_003a}

**to: aPoint**

Set the ending point of the arc in cartesian coordinates
